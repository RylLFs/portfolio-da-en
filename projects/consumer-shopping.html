Visualization and Analysis of Consumer Shopping Trends

Analyzing consumer shopping patterns for accurate business insights

Context / Problem

In this fourth-semester project, I analyzed a consumer shopping transaction dataset obtained from Kaggle, which includes 3,091 rows of data. Through this analysis, I explored purchasing patterns based on time, popular product categories, and the impact of demographic factors on purchasing decisions. Through interactive visualizations and a comprehensive dashboard in Power BI, actionable insights are presented to support strategic decision-making in inventory management and more effective marketing.

Key Features & Insight

Interactive Dashboard

Dynamic dashboard with multidimensional filters allowing in-depth exploration of sales trends by category, region, and time period.

Trend Analysis

Identification of seasonal patterns and monthly sales fluctuations for optimizing promotional strategies and inventory management.

Revenue Recommendations

Data-driven recommendations for increasing average transaction value, customer retention, and revenue per segment.

Insights

Based on the data visualization results, one insight is that purchases are dominated by men at 68% and women at 32%, with the clothing segment being the most sold.
